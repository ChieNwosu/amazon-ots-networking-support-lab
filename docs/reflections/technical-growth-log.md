## How to Add Entries

When adding a new entry, include:

1. **Date:** Month and year (YYYY-MM format)
2. **Milestone:** Concise description of what was achieved
3. **Course / Activity:** What course, lab, or activity produced this milestone
4. **Reflection:** One or two sentences on what was learned or why it matters

Examples of future milestones to document:

- Completing a course module or specialization
- Earning a certificate
- Successfully completing a hands-on lab
- Writing a new runbook based on course material
- Achieving a specific score on a practice exam
- Making a connection between course content and FC operations
